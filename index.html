Fall trends

This is always a popular topic— seasonal trends, so here it is, guys!

Here I have four popular hairstyles, colours, cuts, and overall looks for women's autumn hair trends for 2013.

I'll be posting a shorter blurb for men's look this Fall/Winter soon.

What's trending:

  1. Middle part with long, slightly-layered wavy hair.
  2. Whispy side-bangs matched with a long bob.
  3. Ombre is still trending but in deeper, auburn-y brown tones or golden rich blondes.
  4. Chestnut browns, golden deep blondes, subtle peek-a-boo tones on deeper brunettes.
  5. The top-knot or large bun is still a go this season because of it's simplicity and femininity.
