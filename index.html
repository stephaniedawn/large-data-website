One girl's thoughts on all things hairstyling.

For information, tips, tricks, and tutorials on all things hair-related.

Hi, I am Stephanie Dawn. I am a young stylist who lives in Ottawa, Ontario, and I have been a hairstylist for 3 years now. This site is mainly a blog about my personal experiences in the salon industry, the knowledge I have gained from that, tips I have picked up from other stylists, and opinions on specific styles, products, and tools.

Fall Hair Trends

This is always a popular topic— seasonal hair trends, so here it is, guys!

Here I have four popular hairstyles, colours, cuts, and overall looks for women's autumn hair trends for 2013.

I'll be posting a blog for men's look this Fall/Winter soon.

What's trending:

  1. Middle part with long, slightly-layered wavy hair.
  2. Whispy side-bangs matched with a long bob.
  3. Ombre is still trending but in deeper, auburn-y brown tones or golden rich blondes.
  4. Chestnut browns, golden deep blondes, subtle peek-a-boo tones on deeper brunettes.
  5. The top-knot or large bun is still a go this season because of it's simplicity and femininity.
